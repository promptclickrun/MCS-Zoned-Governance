Governance Zones

Three Zones, Three Levels of Oversight

Each zone maps to a distinct risk profile, environment type, and ownership model — giving every agent a clear governance path.

Zone 1
GREEN ZONE
Safe Innovation

Citizen developers build and manage personal agents with safe defaults. Admins stay hands-off.

Key Indicators
  • ✓ Personal Developer environment
  • ✓ M365 data sources only
  • ✓ Personal or small workgroup audience
  • ✓ No external connectors needed
  • ✓ Maker owns full lifecycle
  • ✓ Low complexity, no autonomous actions
Developer Env Personal Use Low Risk
Zone 2
YELLOW ZONE
Guided Development

Makers lead development with active IT guidance. Agents expand beyond personal use into departmental scenarios.

Key Indicators
  • ! Trial, Sandbox, or limited Production environment
  • ! External connectors or APIs required
  • ! Department-level sharing (up to ~99 users)
  • ! IT guidance and approval needed
  • ! Custom DLP policies required
  • ! Transitioning from experimentation to production
Sandbox Env Dept Use Medium Risk
Zone 3
RED ZONE
IT Managed

IT owns the full agent lifecycle. Enterprise-wide deployment requiring full ALM, security reviews, and professional development.

Key Indicators
  • ✕ IT-provisioned Production environment
  • ✕ Enterprise-wide or tenant catalog publishing
  • ✕ Sensitive data, multi-agent orchestration, or autonomous actions
  • ✕ Full ALM pipeline (Dev → Test → Prod)
  • ✕ Privacy, RAI, SDL, Threat Modeling reviews
  • ✕ Pro-dev or IT-approved makers only
Prod Env Enterprise High Risk
